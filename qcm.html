Copy Methods

Copy From: Standard Templates, WCP Daily, Outages, Flagged WCP

Copy from Standard Template

to WCP Daily

Step 1 - Select WCP(s) To Copy , Use Cntrl Click To multi select, Click + swipe and release or Click, Shift Click to select.

Step 2 - Click Copy To: Work Clearance Permit Area. Click Ok Or Cancel to system responce.

Copy from Standard Template

to WCP Outage

Step 1 - Select WCP(s) To Copy , Use Cntrl Click To multi select, Click + swipe and release or Click, Shift Click to select.

Step 2 - Click Copy To: Outage after selecting on an Active or Template outage from dropdown. Click Ok Or Cancel to system responce.

Copy Flagged WCP Daily

To WCP Standard - Site Admin Only

Step 1 - Select WCP that has been flagged to copy. The TYPE field will show FLG.

Step 2 - Click Make Standard to copy WCP into WCP Standard Template Area. Type field will be changed to STD to show it became a Standard template.
